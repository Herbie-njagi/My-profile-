PURPOSE

"Purpose is not just a direction—it’s a destiny whispered by God into your spirit."

In life, we are often distracted by trends, trapped by comparison, and slowed by fear. But purpose is the compass that redirects us. It defines not only where you're going, but why you’re here.

Every vision I’ve birthed—Ground Snitch, RentEase, RV Scan, this website—was rooted in one question: “What was I born to solve?”

You are not random. You are a strategy. A response to a problem in the earth. Walk in it boldly.
